**Version:** 1.0 | **Date:** April 21, 2026 | **Status:** Active

## Executive Summary

AccrediTex is a cloud-based, bilingual (EN/AR), AI-powered SaaS accreditation management platform targeting hospitals, laboratories, and healthcare networks in the MENA region and globally. The platform is production-ready at https://accreditex.web.app with 19 fully built modules, iOS + Android mobile apps, and 28 pre-built HIS/LIMS integration connectors.

**Primary Goal:** Acquire first 50 paying hospital accounts within 12 months.  
**Secondary Goal:** Establish AccrediTex as the #1 recognized accreditation platform brand in the GCC.

### Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

**Primary Buyer:** Accreditation Manager / Quality Manager  
- Pain: Manual spreadsheet-based prep, document chaos, audit anxiety  
- Trigger: Upcoming survey (3–12 months out), previous accreditation failure  
- Budget Authority: Medium (recommends, doesn't approve)  
- Decision Timeline: 30–90 days with IT/management approval  

**Economic Buyer:** Hospital CEO / COO / CMO  
- Pain: Risk of accreditation failure, staff productivity waste, lack of visibility  
- Trigger: Board pressure, patient safety incident, competitive hospital gets accredited  
- Budget Authority: Full  
- Decision Timeline: 60–120 days  

**Technical Buyer:** IT Director / CTO  
- Pain: Integration complexity, data security, mobile access for field staff  
- Decision Criteria: Security compliance, API availability, HIS connectivity, mobile

### Channel 1: LinkedIn (Primary Digital Channel)
**Target:** Healthcare quality titles in GCC  
**Tactics:**
- 3× weekly posts (feature spotlights, pain points, case studies)
- Sponsored InMail to QA Managers and Hospital CEOs (500 contacts/month)
- LinkedIn Sales Navigator for prospect identification
- Thought leadership articles from accreditation experts

**Monthly Budget:** $2,000–$3,000  
**Expected MQL/month:** 20–30

### Channel 2: Direct Sales (Outbound)
**Target:** 500 GCC hospitals  
**Tactics:**
- Email sequence (3-touch — see content-creator.md)
- LinkedIn connection + InMail combo
- Conference follow-up sequences
- Cold call for enterprise accounts (>200 beds)

**Monthly Target:** 50 outreach contacts → 5 demos → 1 close  
**Expected Conversion Rate:** 2% outreach → close

**Budget per Conference:** $8,000–$15,000  
**Expected leads per event:** 30–80

**Tactics:**

### Channel 5: Partner / Reseller Channel

## 9. Sales Process

```
Awareness → Lead → MQL → Demo → Pilot → Close → Onboard

1. AWARENESS: LinkedIn post / conference / Google search
2. LEAD: Visits accreditex.web.app, signs up for free trial or contacts sales
3. MQL: Engages with 2+ pages or requests demo
4. DEMO: 30-min live demo (screen share, live platform)
5. PILOT: 30-day free pilot in their environment
6. CLOSE: Proposal sent, contract signed
7. ONBOARD: 2-week implementation + training
```

**Sales Cycle Length:** 30–90 days (Starter), 60–180 days (Enterprise)
