Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 17, 2026  |  Last Updated: April 17, 2026  |  عربي

AccrediTex ("we", "our", or "us") is operated by AccrediTex FZ-LLC, a company registered in the Abu Dhabi Global Market (ADGM), United Arab Emirates. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our accreditation management platform.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Data: Name, work email address, job title, organization name, and password (hashed via Firebase Authentication).
  • Organization Data: Healthcare facility details, accreditation program selections, subscription plan, and billing contact.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, audit logs, and activity timestamps — used for security monitoring and product improvement.
  • Payment Data: Processed exclusively by Stripe, Inc. We store only your Stripe Customer ID and subscription status — never raw card numbers.
  • AI Interaction Data: Prompts and responses generated via our AI assistant, associated with your user account and organization.
  • Technical Data: Browser type, IP address, and session identifiers for security and fraud prevention.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Deliver, operate, and maintain the AccrediTex platform.
  • Process subscription payments and manage your account.
  • Provide AI-assisted accreditation support and compliance recommendations.
  • Send transactional emails (account verification, invoices, alerts) — not marketing without consent.
  • Monitor platform security and detect unauthorized access.
  • Comply with UAE ADGM Data Protection Regulations 2021 and applicable health data laws.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Firebase (Google LLC): Authentication and cloud database hosting — covered by Google's Data Processing Agreement.
  • Stripe, Inc.: Payment processing — governed by Stripe's Privacy Policy.
  • OpenAI / AI providers: Prompts may be processed by our AI backend; we do not send patient-identifiable data.
  • Legal authorities: If required by UAE law or a valid court order.

4. Data Retention

We retain your account data for the duration of your subscription plus 12 months. Audit logs are retained for 7 years to meet healthcare accreditation requirements. You may request deletion of non-legally-required data at any time.

5. Your Rights (ADGM DIFC Aligned)

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data.
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate data.
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your account and data (subject to legal retention requirements).
  • Portability: Export your data in JSON or CSV format.
  • Objection: Object to processing for marketing purposes.

To exercise any right, email privacy@accreditex.io. We respond within 30 days.

6. Security

All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest via Firebase/Google Cloud encryption. Access is restricted by role-based multi-tenancy controls. We conduct regular security audits.

7. Cookies

We use strictly necessary cookies for authentication session management and functional cookies for UI preferences (dark mode, language). We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. You can withdraw consent for non-essential cookies via our cookie banner.

8. Children's Privacy

AccrediTex is a B2B platform for healthcare professionals. We do not knowingly collect data from individuals under 18.

9. International Transfers

Data may be processed by Firebase (Google) servers in the EU and US under Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs). We ensure equivalent protection standards apply.

10. Changes to This Policy

We will notify registered users by email at least 14 days before material changes take effect. Continued use after the effective date constitutes acceptance.
