# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability

The project team and community take all security vulnerabilities seriously. Thank you for improving the security of our project. We appreciate your efforts and responsible disclosure and will make every effort to acknowledge your contributions.

Report security vulnerabilities by emailing the project lead at [ashraf@ashrafmusa.com](mailto:ashraf@ashrafmusa.com).

The project lead will acknowledge your email within 48 hours, and will send a more detailed response within 48 hours indicating the next steps in handling your report. After the initial reply to your report, the security team will endeavor to keep you informed of the progress towards a fix and full announcement, and may ask for additional information or guidance.

Report security vulnerabilities in third-party modules to the person or team maintaining the module.

## Incident Response Process

In case of a security incident, the project lead will follow these steps:

1.  **Triage**: The project lead will investigate the reported vulnerability to determine its severity and impact.
2.  **Fix**: The project lead will work on a fix for the vulnerability.
3.  **Release**: The project lead will release a new version of the project with the fix.
4.  **Disclosure**: The project lead will disclose the vulnerability to the community.
